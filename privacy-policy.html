Privacy Policy for CPM Project Management App

Last Updated: 29-06-2025

Thank you for using CPM-Net web application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information Collection and Use

No Personal Data Collection:
This application runs entirely in your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All project data (tasks, dependencies, etc.) is stored locally on your device or managed via your browser’s file system.

Data Export:
If you choose to export your project, a file is generated and downloaded to your computer. This process does not involve any data being sent to us or to any third party.

Import/Open File:
When you import or open a project file, the data is read locally from your device and processed in your browser only.

2. Cookies and Tracking

No Cookies or Tracking:
This application does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. No information about your usage is collected or shared.

3. Third-Party Services

GitHub Pages:
This app is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please review GitHub’s Privacy Policy for information on how GitHub may collect data related to hosting.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the repository issue tracker or the contact method provided on the project page.

By using this application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
